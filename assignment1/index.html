About IET DAVV

The Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET) at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, is a premier engineering institute established in 1996. The institute offers cutting-edge courses in various engineering disciplines, with a focus on research, innovation, and academic excellence.
